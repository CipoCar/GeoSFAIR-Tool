**GeoSFAIR (GeoSciences products FAIR evaluation for GeoSpatial data FAIR evaluation)**

GeoSFAIR is a Java-based application designed to measure and certify the level of adherence to the FAIR principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable) for research products within the Geosciences domain.

The tool stands out for its ability to manage the complexity of geospatial data. In its evaluation process, GeoSFAIR goes beyond generic standards by integrating the technical requirements of national and European regulations:

- AgID Guidelines: ensuring consistency with the national repertoire of spatial data.

- INSPIRE Directive: guaranteeing full interoperability of geographic data at the European level.
