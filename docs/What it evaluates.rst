What it evaluates?
==================

The GeoSFAIR tool can evaluate various research products, from traditional **metadata** and **datasets** to support products such as **vocabularies** for improving the semantic quality of data and products in general. It can also assess compliance with certain FAIR principles in **software code** developed for applications, tools, or plugins that contain analysis models or methodologies. It can also assess whether the metainformation associated with the distribution of a **document** complies with OpenAIRE standards and, consequently, the FAIR principles. 
It should be noted that documents include:

- scientific papers in journals and books; - unrefereed scientific papers; 
- technical documents (guidelines, tutorials, best practices, reports); 
- geological and geothematic maps (digital PDF format). 

The tool's primary objective is to evaluate geospatial datasets and their metadata, taking into account European regulations (including national ones, where applicable) and international standards such as ISO, W3C, OGC, and IUGS-CGI.
The tool also offers the ability to comprehensively evaluate the entire production chain associated with a scientific product, including the dataset, its metadata, and all the vocabularies used to enrich the dataset and metadata, thus obtaining a complete electronic printout of the product's FAIRness.
